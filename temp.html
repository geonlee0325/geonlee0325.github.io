About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI at KAIST, advised by Prof. Kijung Shin at the Data Mining Lab. I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Sungkyunkwan University. I have also interned at Snap Research, NEC Labs America, and Amazon. My research interests include graph mining & learning, recommender systems, time series analysis, multi‑modal learning, and social network analysis.
